2015 in review

A year of big changes and even bigger adventures.

New year. New job. New state.

First of all, welcome to my version of "year end holiday car update about Shawn". As some of you may already know, last year was a huge year for me. It included a big move across the country as well as a bunch of exciting family events.

Silicon Valley

Probably the biggest change for me in 2015 was the move out to California for my new job. 2015 marked 3 years with Sogeti and I decided that it was time to move on and see what else was out there. During a trip to California to visit a good friend, I was introduced to Dynamic Signal and proceeded to pursue a career out in Silicon Valley. The move wasn't easy as it put me thousands of miles away from my family but they have been nothing but supportive and the impact it has had on my career has been outstanding.

I currently live in Palo Alto, home of Stanford University, and about 30 minutes south of San Francisco. Since I don't need one, I got rid of my car in favor or riding CalTrain to get around, which is a huge change from what I was used to in Indiana

Weddings and such

This year I was honored to be in my brother's wedding. The weather was beautifully hot and it was really awesome to see the two families come together and celebrate.

Star Wars 7-Movie Marathon

For some reason I thought it would be a good idea to participate in a 7-movie marathon leading up to the release of the newest Star Wars film. Needless to say, it was an experience and well worth it. That being said, I don't believe I will ever be doing that again.

Flat Stanley

While in California, I want to make sure I take advantage of everything the West Coast has to offer while I'm here. In 2015, my nephew Graham's class participated in the Flat Stanley program and sent his out to visit me. This gave me even more incentive to travel around the area and visit places like Big Sur, Golden Gate Park, and the Santa Cruz boardwalk.
